You are given three integers $a$, $b$, and $c$. Return $a+b+c$.

---

##### 제한사항

* $0 \le a \le 10^{18}$
* $0 \le b \le 10^{18}$
* $0 \le c \le 10^{18}$

---

##### 입출력 예

| a   | b   | c   | result |
| --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1   | 2   | 3   | 6      |

---

##### 입출력 예 설명

입출력 예 #1

* $a+b+c = 1+2+3 = 6$
